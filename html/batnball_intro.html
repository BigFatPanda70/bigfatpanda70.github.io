Bat'n'Ball

The object of the game is to clear each screen frull of bricks using a ball, and a bat which you can move left or right

You have 3 lives to get as high as score as possible

Extra lives are awarded as your score reaches a certain amount

Click on the image to play

Controls:


Keyboard:

Move left : key A,J, or use the left arrow key.

Move right : key D,L, or use the right arrow key.

If you are lucky enough to have a keyboard press 'P' to pause the game.


Touch screens:

Move left : press the left side of the screen

Move right : press the right side of the screen


Joystick support:

Is available for the Firefox web browser.
